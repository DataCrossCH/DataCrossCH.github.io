We do good with data

Our purpose

We bring data science skills to groups who need it.

DataCross partners with socially-conscious organisations who have data to provide low- and no-cost data analytics.

We build teams of volunteer data scientists, define and manage projects, and ensure monitoring and implementation is built-in to deployment.

We help NGOs and NFPs make the best use of their data for social benefit.

Our approach

We match Orgs and projects with volunteers and donors, to shepherd projects from start to finish.

We work hand-in-hand with Orgs to ideate and define their most valuable — potentially lifesaving — AI projects.

Funding from our sponsoring partners is used to gather and organise the necessary data, and to oversee implementation by a team of data scientists.

We make sure that models are deployed, updated and monitored.

Our partners

Our partners are Data Scientists who want to do good for the world.

Our partners are Orgs that have data — but don’t know how to leverage it to help their Mission.

Our partners are socially-minded institutions who want to use their resources to assist the next generation of Data-driven philanthropy.
